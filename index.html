✦ What SafeHer Does

Built with love.
Designed for protection.

🆘

One-Tap SOS

A single tap triggers an emergency alert even on a locked screen. No unlocking, no fumbling — instant help.

📍

Live Location Sharing

Your real-time GPS coordinates are sent to all trusted contacts and authorities the moment SOS is activated.

Core Feature
🎙️

Covert Audio Recording

Silently records ambient audio after SOS. Saved securely as evidence in the cloud for authorities.

👭

Trusted Circle

Add up to 10 trusted people — family, friends, campus security. All receive instant SMS + app notifications.

🤖

Fake Call Feature

Feeling unsafe but can't act? Trigger a fake incoming call that lets you walk away from any uncomfortable situation.

🗺️

Safe Route Planner

AI-powered route suggestions that avoid poorly-lit streets and high-incident zones based on community reports.

🔔

Check-In Reminders

Set timed check-ins. If you don't confirm you're safe in time, an automatic SOS is sent on your behalf.

📊

Incident Heatmap

Community-powered safety heatmap shows zones with high reported incidents so you can plan your journey wisely.

✦ The Process

From danger to safety
in seconds.

01

Sign Up & Build Your Circle

Create your profile, verify your phone number, and add trusted contacts. Set your emergency message and preferred authorities.

02

Activate SOS — One Tap

Press the SOS button (or shake the phone 3 times). An alert triggers instantly, even from the lock screen.

03

Contacts & Authorities Are Notified

All trusted contacts receive an SMS + push notification with your live location link. Campus security is pinged automatically.

04

Live Tracking Begins

Contacts can track your movement in real-time on a shared map until you mark yourself safe or help arrives.

✓

You're Safe — Incident Logged

Mark yourself safe. Incident details, audio recordings, and location trail are securely stored as a report.
